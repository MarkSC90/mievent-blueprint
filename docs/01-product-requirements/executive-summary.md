# Executive Summary

MiEvent is a modern Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to transform how professional event businesses manage, collaborate and deliver events.

The platform replaces fragmented planning processes built around spreadsheets, PDFs, emails and messaging applications with a single collaborative workspace that updates in real time.

Unlike traditional event management software, MiEvent has been designed as a **Workspace-first**, **API-first** platform where every stakeholder involved in an event collaborates from a shared source of truth.

Every event becomes a digital workspace.

Every stakeholder sees the information they need.

Every update is immediately available to authorised users.

---

# Why MiEvent Exists

The professional events industry remains heavily dependent on disconnected software.

A typical event organiser today may use:

- Microsoft Excel
- Microsoft Word
- PDFs
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Google Drive
- Dropbox
- Printed paperwork

Each document is duplicated.

Each update creates another version.

Each supplier receives different information.

Clients frequently request updated schedules.

Venues work from outdated floor plans.

Suppliers receive revised timelines via email.

These inefficiencies increase administrative workload, reduce confidence and introduce unnecessary operational risk.

MiEvent exists to eliminate this fragmentation.

---

# The Problem

Current event planning suffers from five fundamental problems.

## 1. Multiple Sources of Truth

Information exists in many different places.

Guest lists.

Timelines.

Menus.

Floor plans.

Supplier details.

Contracts.

No single location contains the current version.

This creates confusion throughout the planning process.

---

## 2. Excessive Administration

Event organisers spend significant time performing administrative work rather than planning exceptional events.

Examples include:

- Updating spreadsheets
- Sending revised PDFs
- Emailing suppliers
- Chasing confirmations
- Updating seating plans
- Resending schedules

Many of these activities create no additional value for the client.

---

## 3. Poor Collaboration

Communication often relies upon email threads.

Information becomes fragmented.

Important updates are missed.

Decisions become difficult to track.

Responsibility becomes unclear.

---

## 4. Limited Visibility

Clients rarely understand the current status of planning.

Suppliers receive limited context.

Venues lack visibility into evolving requirements.

Organisers become the bottleneck for information.

---

## 5. Software Fragmentation

Most businesses combine multiple software products.

CRM

Documents

Email

Accounting

Planning

Storage

Messaging

Each application stores separate information.

None provide a complete operational picture.

---

# The MiEvent Solution

MiEvent introduces a new operating model.

Every event becomes a collaborative Workspace.

Rather than exchanging documents, stakeholders interact directly with structured information inside the platform.

Permissions ensure each participant only sees the information relevant to them.

Changes become immediately visible.

Every action is recorded.

Every decision becomes traceable.

---

# Product Philosophy

Every design and engineering decision within MiEvent should reinforce the following principles.

## One Workspace

Every event exists within a dedicated collaborative workspace.

Everything relating to that event belongs inside the workspace.

---

## One Source of Truth

There is only one current version of information.

Documents are replaced by structured data.

Users collaborate rather than duplicate.

---

## Collaboration First

The platform is designed around collaboration rather than communication.

Instead of sending updates, users make updates.

The system distributes those updates automatically.

---

## Beautiful Software

MiEvent should feel comparable to the best SaaS products available.

Design inspiration includes:

- Stripe
- Linear
- Vercel
- Notion
- Revolut

The interface should remain calm, spacious and intuitive.

---

## Open Platform

Everything available within the web application must also be accessible through a documented OpenAPI interface.

This allows:

- Website integrations
- CRM integrations
- Mobile applications
- AI assistants
- Accounting software
- Automation platforms

MiEvent should become the operational platform around which other systems integrate.
